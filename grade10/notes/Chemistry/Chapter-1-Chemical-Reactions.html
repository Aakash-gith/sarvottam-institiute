What is a Chemical Reaction?

A chemical reaction is a process where one or more substances (reactants) are transformed into different substances (products). In a chemical reaction, bonds are broken and new bonds are formed.

Characteristics of a Chemical Reaction:
  • Change in color
  • Evolution of gas
  • Change in temperature
  • Formation of precipitate
  • Change in physical state

Balancing Chemical Equations

In a balanced equation, the number of atoms of each element is the same on both sides. This is done by adding coefficients (not changing subscripts).

Steps to Balance an Equation:
  • List the number of each type of atom on both sides
  • Find atoms that are not balanced
  • Adjust coefficients to balance
  • Check that all atoms are balanced
Law of Conservation of Mass: Matter is neither created nor destroyed in a chemical reaction
